## What To Tell Evaluators

- This plugin adds a Filament-native control plane for RAG assistants and embeddable chat widgets.
- It helps teams manage bots, sources, ingestion, retrieval settings, and conversations from inside a Laravel + Filament app.
- It can power a sellable AI feature or assistant product, but it does not replace the rest of the product stack such as billing, tenancy, or domain-specific workflows.
- The best starting points are the product overview for positioning, Quickstart for setup, and Operations for production readiness.

## Good Short Pitch

If you need a short one-paragraph description, use this:

Filament RAG gives a Laravel + Filament app a production-ready chatbot control plane. You can manage bots, sources, ingestion, retrieval settings, widget embeds, and conversations from Filament, while keeping answers grounded in your own content instead of relying on a generic AI wrapper.
